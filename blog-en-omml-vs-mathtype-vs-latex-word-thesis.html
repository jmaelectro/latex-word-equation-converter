OMML vs MathType vs LaTeX in Word: what to choose for thesis submissions

A clear comparison of the three most common equation formats in Word: native OMML, MathType, and LaTeX input. Pros, cons, and a practical recommendation.

Quick comparison

OMML (native Word equations)

  • Editable in Word without add-ins.
  • Typically the safest for submissions.

MathType

  • Powerful editor, but adds dependency.
  • May behave like an object in some setups.

LaTeX (as input)

  • Fast if you know LaTeX.
  • Pasting LaTeX as text doesn’t create editable equations automatically.

Recommendation

If reviewers must edit in Word, aim for OMML as your final output.
